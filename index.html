WELCOME TO SAHABEE ABASON LTD.

Purbachal Sahabee Abason is a new housing Venture of Sahabee Abason Ltd. All the facilities of city life in the true rural atmosphere. The project is located in near by Purbachal New Town (Sector 22 & 30) and also situated besides 180’ Asian Highway. It’s the proper place to nurture the future of the coming generation. Why Invest in Purbachal?

Purbachal was planned to reduce the pressure of extended population’s living place. Since Dhaka is already a crowded city, it was obvious to plan making an alternative city. This is how Purbachal got a beginning 26 years ago.

Dhaka International Trade Fair

Dhaka international trade fair venue permanently moves to 'Bangladesh-China Friendship Exhibition Center' in Purbachal New Town Sector 21. It is situated on the bank of Shitolokkha river.

Bangabandhu Tri Tower

The stability and advantages of Purbachal city can be a prime reason to invest here. Besides, there are various important institutions going to be placed at Purbachal that will secure one’s investment in Purbachal. Bangladesh is going to enter into the 100 floors’ club since Bangabandhu Tri Tower is going to be made at sector 19 of Purbachal. The name indicating 3 buildings and one of them will be 111 storied. Bangabandhu Square will be made at sector 15.

Sheikh Hasina International Cricket Stadium

Sheikh Hasina International Cricket Stadium or The Boat is an under-construction cricket stadium located in Sector-1 of multi-billion dollar Purbachal New Town on the outskirts of Dhaka, Bangladesh. The stadium is a joint venture of National Sports Council and Bangladesh Cricket Board.

At Purbachal, there will be around 27 community centers. Most of the places will be naturally blessed. There will be modern hospitals, and one of them will contain 800 beds. Modern facilities will be available, and people living there will be able to get treatment there with ease. From a religious point of view, Purbachal will also get priority since there will be almost 97 mosques, 5 temples, and 4 other religious places of worship.

The American embassy and other important consulates will be located in the proposed diplomatic zone in the Purbachal region, and the government has pledged to make the area even tougher and safer. A new fire department HQ will be built, two police lines, four police stations, and 24-hour security will be provided for the entire region. Purbachal New Town has become somewhat of a golden goose because of the planned business zones, residential zones, diplomatic zones, stadiums, parks, and entertainment zones. The American Embassy and some other important institutions will be located in the diplomatic zone of the Purbachal region.
