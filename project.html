Why Select “Purbachal Sahabee Abason” Trade Center

  • A tremendous investment opportunity maximizing profit more than 200% on land purchase value within next 3 years.

  • CHAYABITHI consists of both Residential and Commercial Plots with all facilities for all class of people with own identity.
  • A number of Professional Engineers, Town Planners, Architects and Environmentalists formed a team to design the well-organized layout plan of “Purbachal Sahabee Abason”.
  • “Purbachal Sahabee Abason” will avail & enjoy all facilities of Govt.’s project ' Purbachal New Town' because of its identical location.
  • A small township having a pure and natural greeneries, landscape
  • The “Purbachal Sahabee Abason” is free from & away of Dhaka-City-Air-pollution caused by crippling traffic jams
  • To make Environment Friendly Project, 30%of total land will be reserved for Ecological Balance Greeneries, Lakes and Open Spaces etc.
